About Me

I am an enthusiastic IT undergraduate with a strong passion for web and mobile application development. With hands-on experience in both front-end and back-end technologies, I enjoy crafting seamless, user-friendly digital experiences that make life easier and more engaging.

Over the years, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React and Tailwind CSS. I also develop mobile apps using React Native and Flutter, ensuring responsive and performant applications across multiple devices.

Beyond coding, I am deeply fascinated by emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning. I have worked on AI-powered projects that showcase my ability to integrate smart algorithms with practical applications.

Continuous learning and collaboration are my core values. I actively contribute to open source projects, participate in coding challenges, and stay up-to-date with the latest tech trends to keep sharpening my skills. My goal is to build impactful software solutions that solve real-world problems.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy reading tech blogs, experimenting with new tools, and mentoring fellow developers. I’m excited to connect with like-minded professionals and contribute to innovative projects.
